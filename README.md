## About

I am a **Robotics & Artificial Intelligence engineer** (B.S. in progress at Jadara University, expected 2028) working at the intersection of autonomous systems, deep reinforcement learning, and full-stack product engineering. I build **autonomous AI agents**, **intelligent control systems**, and **neural architectures** — from RL agents that plan in Keplerian orbital space to async multi-agent frameworks published on PyPI.

As **Chairman of the IEEE Computational Intelligence Society (CIS)** at Jadara University, I lead technical workshops, training, and community programs in applied AI, machine learning, and robotics.
